Kirk Loves his family and his Culture

Both sides of Kirk's family tree migrated from Jamaica to America in the early 1970s. Kirk was born in Jamaica however he moved to the United States at the age of four months old. A majority of Kirk's family now reside in America but they still carry the strong roots of their upbringing in Jamaica deep in their hearts. The Jamaican national motto is 'Out of Many One People', based on the population's multiracial roots. Kirk and his family truly believe this motto, they all carry the humanitarian spirit to help others regardless of race, class, or creed.
Detailed below are some fun facts about Jamaica, and what it is well know for:
Reggae music originated in Jamaica, home of well known musician Bob Marley
In Jamaica they drive on the left-hand side of the road
Many talented track and field athletes call Jamaica home, namely one of the fastest men alive, Usain Bolt
Jamaicans know how to have fun anywhere they are

Kirk loves cars

Growing up in Queens, NY, which he also reps to the fullest, he discovered his love for cars.

Kirk loves cars because they are fun to drive, and nice to look at.
In his spare time Kirk can always be found working on one his cars.
A new set of car tools is a sure way to excite Kirk.
He decided he wanted a career working with cars while he was in high school.
He aspires to open his own used car dealership that offers affordable European cars for sale.
He would enjoy the opportunity of passing his love for cars down to his children.
